# Chief of Staff Skill

My personal Chief of Staff agent to help me keep my work life organized!

Uses MCP tools to pull information from connected work systems (Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Tasks, Google Docs, JIRA, Confluence, Github, Slack) for recent activities 
and summarizes an actionable daily briefing into my Obsidian Vault on my laptop.

Sample tasks:

- "Chief, prep my daily briefing"
- "Chief, process my email inbox and summarize AI meeting notes into my Obsidian Vault"

> [!WARNING]  
> This is shared for inspiration only, rather than to be reused by others. This is a personalized agent that has been specifically adapted to my personal organization system.
> I recommend installing Claude's [Skill Creator plugin](https://code.claude.com/docs/en/skills#run-evals-with-skill-creator) which
> has built-in evaluations so that you can adapt it to your specific needs and verify the output matches your expectations.
> Enjoy!
